# Privacy Policy for AI Dream Scope

## Introduction

Welcome to AI Dream Scope, a platform dedicated to helping you interpret and understand your dreams. At AI Dream Scope, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our dream interpretation services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with personalized dream interpretation services:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Your name, email address, and any information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide customer support, and personalize your dream interpretation experience.

2. **Dream Content**
   - **What We Collect**: The dreams you submit for interpretation.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze and provide meaningful interpretations of your dreams.

3. **Usage Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you interact with our website, including features accessed and frequency of use.
   - **Purpose**: To improve our dream interpretation services and enhance user experience.

4. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Data about the devices you use to access AI Dream Scope, such as device type, operating system, and browser.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our website for different devices and ensure compatibility.

5. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our website and provide a more personalized experience.

6. **Payment Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods for premium dream interpretation services, such as credit card details and billing address.
   - **Purpose**: To process payments for our services.

## Data Storage and Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information and dream content. All data we collect is stored securely on our servers with appropriate safeguards. We implement various security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. However, please note that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, safety, or that of our users or others.
- To trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website or providing our dream interpretation services, subject to confidentiality agreements.

## Your Dream Content

We understand that dreams can be deeply personal. While we need access to your dream content to provide interpretation services, we treat this information with the utmost confidentiality. Your dream submissions are used solely for the purpose of providing you with interpretations and insights.
